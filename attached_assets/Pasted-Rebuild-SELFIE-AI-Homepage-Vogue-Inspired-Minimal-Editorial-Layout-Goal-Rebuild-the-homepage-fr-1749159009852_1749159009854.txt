Rebuild SELFIE AI™ Homepage (Vogue-Inspired, Minimal Editorial Layout)
Goal: Rebuild the homepage from scratch following this exact UX/UI brief. It should feel like a luxury editorial magazine meets personal brand studio. Clean, minimal, scroll-based storytelling. All overlay text in white or black only. No emojis or icons. No round corners. Think Vogue x Chanel meets digital empowerment.

1. 🌌 Global Layout Rules
Backgrounds: White or #171719 (Luxury Black)

Typography:

Headline: Cormorant Garamond, serif (use italics on all quotes + H2)

Body + nav + CTAs: Neue Einstellung, sans-serif

Font Sizes (Desktop):

H1: 64px / 400

H2: 36px italic

Paragraph: 18px

Button: 14px uppercase, 0.5px spacing

Buttons: Transparent with sharp black or white border (depending on background), all-caps, no icons

All Cards: Flat, no shadow, scale-up on hover

Image Filters: Use grayscale or desaturated tone for subtlety—but do not make all B&W
