🚀 30-60-90 DAY IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
Let’s turn your story into a scroll-stopping, money-making brand—step by step.

🖤 FIRST 30 DAYS: Build the Foundation
This month is all about clarity and confidence.
We’re getting raw, mapping your journey, and turning it into your positioning power.

Week 1: Write out your full transformation timeline
(Before → Rock Bottom → Turning Point → Glow Up)

Week 2: Use your Emotional Connection Strategy Matrix
(What do you want them to feel when they see you?)

Week 3: Fill out the Authority Position Finder
(Find your angle, your edge, your “because I...” statement)

Week 4: Put it all together into one bold positioning statement
(Your “why me” message that makes people choose you)

🎯 By Day 30: You’ll have a story-driven brand identity that’s powerful, personal, and 100% yours.

✨ DAYS 31–60: Show Up and Test It
Now that you’ve got your message—it’s time to make it visible.

Week 5: Update your bios + website to reflect your new positioning
(Say it loud. Say it proud.)

Week 6: Create 3-5 posts that tell your transformation story
(Use reels, selfies, carousels—whatever feels most you)

Week 7: Draft a signature talk based on your story
(For podcasts, lives, workshops—this is your expert moment)

Week 8: Get real feedback from your audience
(Ask: “What part of my story hit you the most?”)

🎯 By Day 60: People should know exactly what you do—and why you’re the one to follow.

👑 DAYS 61–90: Own It and Expand
You’re not just showing up—you’re leading now.

Week 9: Pitch yourself for 3 podcast interviews
(Use your story as your superpower angle)

Week 10: Create a mini freebie based on your transformation
(Think: “How I went from X to Y” — make it juicy + helpful)

Week 11: Share a few case studies that show your framework in action
(Prove your process works—with results)

Week 12: Launch a content series or live workshop that positions you as the go-to expert
(Bring the fire. Show the receipts.)

🎯 By Day 90: Your brand should feel like a movement.
People should be saying: “She’s the one I’ve been waiting for.”
