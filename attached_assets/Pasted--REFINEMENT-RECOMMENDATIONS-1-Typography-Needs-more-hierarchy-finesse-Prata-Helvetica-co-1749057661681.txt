🖤 REFINEMENT RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Typography:
Needs more hierarchy + finesse.

✅ Prata + Helvetica combo is working.
⚠️ But headers feel default-sized. Too flat.

Upgrade:

H1: Prata, 48–60px, tight letter spacing -1%, uppercase or title case.

Subheads: Helvetica Neue Light, 24px, 300 weight, 1.4 line-height.

Body: 16px max, gray tone #4C4B4B — never full black.

Quote lines: Italic Prata, center aligned, spaced out.

📝 Use size contrast and spacing like a magazine layout. Not just H1, H2, P. Make each line feel styled.

2. Imagery Layout:
Your current layout is clean, but it still feels boxy.

Upgrade:

Use overlapping layers: let text sit partly on images with semi-transparent black/white overlays (like in your inspiration grid).

Add editorial full-bleed hero sections with floating captions.

Use asymmetry. Don’t center everything — offset text and images for visual rhythm.

Use subtle hover zoom-ins on every photo card with beige glow filter.

📌 Your “before and after” grid? Gorgeous. Just soften gridlines and add an editorial caption under it like a fashion ad.

3. Color Application:
✅ Palette is on point
⚠️ Execution could be more intentional.

Upgrade:

Add soft beige underlines, dividers, or background sections (right now it’s very white-heavy)

Apply #B5B5B3 accent lines under quotes or sections

Use #171719 sparingly—right now it dominates too many CTAs/buttons. Use it where you want gravitas.

4. Buttons + Interactions:
Currently look SaaS-y. Not editorial.

Upgrade:

Make primary buttons thinner + wider → more elegant

Font: All caps, 14px, letter-spacing 2%

Padding: 10px 32px

Add animated beige underline on hover instead of full color swap

Use ghost buttons (thin black borders) with white background for calm actions

5. Footer + Quote Section:
Your quote is beautiful, but too subtle.

Upgrade:

Center it in a beige block (use #F5E6DC lightly)

Use Prata Italic, 24px, spaced out

Add a small “editorial divider” under it (like a soft logo or line)

6. Section Spacing:
Spacing is solid but slightly too techy.

Upgrade:

Add 80px+ between major blocks

Always have at least 64px breathing room top/bottom

Use narrow 1-column intro text like a magazine intro paragraph

7. Missing Luxury Touches:
Here’s what will truly bring it into Chanel/Vogue territory:

✅ Add:

Scroll-triggered fadeInUp animations (text and cards)

Full-bleed quote section mid-page: “I didn’t find power in perfection—I found it in showing up.”

Magazine-style numbered headlines: 01. The Studio | 02. Templates | 03. Vault

Hero overlays: text ON photo with slight blur backdrop (backdrop-filter)

Optional: light beige background texture (like paper or linen) at 5% opacity for luxury depth
