💡 Developer/Designer Instructions (in plain language)
Keep it clean, white, and scroll-friendly.

Design should feel luxury, not loud.

Use bold Prata headlines with soft body text below.

No harsh colors, no excessive gradients, no clunky layouts.

Every touchpoint should feel like a mirror: “She sees herself in this.”
